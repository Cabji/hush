Setting Channels

Channels in Hush refer to IRC channels that you want Hush to monitor. The software detects if anyone in a channel is offering XDCC packs for download and will build a database of what is offered. You can find XDCC channels by searching the internet.

To modify your watched channels:

  1. Open the main Hush window by clicking the Hush button on the toolbar in KVIrc (or press Ctrl+Alt+H)
  2. Click the Channels button
      3. To add a channel:
    1. Click inside the text input box and type in the channel name to watch
    2. Click the Add button or press Enter to save the channel to the watched list
      To remove a channel:
    1. Click to highlight channel names to be removed in the watched list (you can highlight multiple items at the same time)
    2. Click the Remove button or press the Delete key or press the Backspace key
  3. Close the Channels window
